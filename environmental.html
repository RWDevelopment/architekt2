Law Courts
Law Courts

The aim of this project is to improve the quality of the space old station. The site has drawn a lot of interest because of the location of footbridge. Now we have an architecture that connects a city divided by the railroad tracks. Industrial character of the place was a huge inspiration for this project. Structure will combine industrial character of the place with modern architecture. Therefore, to integrate development with pedestrian bridge, the footbrige was flip across the old station and multiplied upward. The cuboid form sticks in the net underline direction of tracks. This is surrealistic reference to truck. The development was designed to discover the station and making this building as exhibit.. Design part of the building highlight linear system of existing station. Usage glazing packages for total building cladding to help know to familiarisation with communications inside building from afar. This is in order to encourage of pedestrian to enter. To get to the other side you have to go through interior. In the hallway of the main building the functions divided between exhibition and gastronomic. Also square on the other side of the station is design as cinematic square and area for recreation. The location of gastronomic local is to promote integration of local community. The shortest way up here's through the building on walk from the city center. The project this is strict and straight solid figure. Steel beams of footh bridge, metal netting of reiling and hanging truck this is the continuation of existing infrastructure. The strict, spacious and open interior design, is to correspond with architecture of a whole building and environment.
